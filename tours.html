East Africa's Mara, Crater & Serengeti Migration

One of our best African tours, this 9-day safari kicks off in Kenya's Masai Mara before heading south into Tanzania for the Serengeti, Lake Manyara and the Ngorongoro Crater. Focused on wildlife, this is a great opportunity to witness the wildebeest migration so ask us about getting your timing right.

Tour highlights

  1. Unmatched viewing of the Big 5
  2. Collection of Africa's best lodges
  3. Day & night game drives
  4. Witness the Great Migration in full swing
  5. Nature walks & mountain bike experiences
